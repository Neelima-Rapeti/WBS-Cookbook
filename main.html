Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F. Grease and flour a bread loaf pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, cream together 1⁄2 cup softened butter and 3⁄4 cup sugar
  3. Mash bananas with a fork until the consistency of chunky applesauce and add them to the batter along with 2 eggs, mixing until blended.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together: 11⁄2 cups of flour, 1 tsp of baking soda and 1⁄2 tsp of salt then add to batter.
  5. Add 1⁄2 tsp of vanilla extract. Pour into prepared loaf pan. Bake at 350˚F for 55-60 min or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Enjoy!!
